About Me

I am a game designer with a passion for creating engaging and immersive gaming experiences. I have a strong background in game mechanics, level design, and player experience. My goal is to contribute to innovative projects that push the boundaries of interactive entertainment.

With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of player psychology, I strive to design games that are not only fun to play but also meaningful and memorable. I am always eager to learn new skills and techniques to enhance my craft.

In addition to game design, I am heavily into creative writing—especially short stories—and general programming in C#, Python, and C++.
